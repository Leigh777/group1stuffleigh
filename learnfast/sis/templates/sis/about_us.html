Learn Fast Mission

The Tutoring-Learning Center’s mission is to offer student-centered academic
support across disciplines. In cooperation with faculty and staff, the center
provides peer-to-peer assistance through individual and small group collaboration.

Learn Fast Vision

The Tutoring-Learning Center staff strives to maximize academic potential and
promote student success in a safe and welcoming environment. We adhere to
the following views of learning:

  1. An inclusive environment is integral to a successful education.
  2. Every student brings unique abilities to the classroom.
  3. Each discipline has a unique way of reading, writing, and learning.
  4. Learning is not generic; learning always occurs in a specific context.
  5. Active learning improves a student’s ability to be successful and leads to student ownership of learning.
  6. Collaboration encourages the development of critical thinking and benefits the learner.
